ABOUT RGF

OUR PROCESS

The RGF Service Process is a 3-step living Service Excellence process that borrows from industry-recognised standards; which we continuously improve to incorporate best practices from business and HR disciplines.

Finding your Wants

Identifying where you are, where you want to be and how to get there may not be easy. At RGF, our consultants are here to help you clarify your next career direction through understanding your personality and bigger aspirations, looking beyond your skills and experience. Helping you find the next best career match.

Understand

What we do:

  • career consultancy
  • candidate scouting
  • internal interview

Making the Connection

Job search no longer has to be a stroke of luck. In this phase, we bridge you with your ideal employer - working with you to prepare for interviews, including giving you background on the interviewer's character, corporate culture and possible line of questions. Having the trusted relationship with front-line managers, we can come in as your first-degree referer.

Bridge

What we do:

  • jobs matching
  • client-candidate introduction
  • interview advice

Closing the Gaps

We make job offer and closing process sweeter - leave the sensitive role of compensation negotiation to our experienced consultants to strike the best balance between you and your prospective employer. Upon successful placement, our consultants can advice you how to leave their current employer on good terms and support you to adapt into your new role.

Support

What we do:

  • offer and closing process
  • candidate support and follow-up
