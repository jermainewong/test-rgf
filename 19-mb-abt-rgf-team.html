Careers with RGF

Building our team has been one of the key priorities at RGF. We're always looking to engage team-focused, results-driven entrepreneurs who share a passion in working with people.

In the business for people, the role of a recruiter is to act as a consultant to both corporate organisations and candidates. You will utilize your knowledge of the business and market environment when meeting both clients and candidates; in understanding the culture of the organisation; and in realizing the long-term career goals and expectations of the candidate.

This rewarding opportunity requires a passionate individual with the drive to succeed in a functional, target-based, customer-interfacing role. If you are a person with strong entrepreneurial ability, drive and ambition, and believe in teamwork, then the recruitment field will be an ideal match for your career.
